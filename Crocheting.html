                                                                                                                 What is Crochet?

                                                                                   Crochet is a type of needlecraft which involves the use of a hooked needle to create fabric from loops of yarn or threads. It is similar to

                                                               knitting, but instead of using 2 needles, crochet uses a single hook. With crochet, you can create a wide variety of items such as blankets, hats, and

                                                               even intricate lacework. It's a hobby enjoyed by many people around the world which offers a fun, creative way to spend your free time.
